Our Story

About Us

Welcome to Paw Care — where your pet’s comfort and happiness come first. We’re a small, dedicated team of animal lovers offering personalized pet care services with a gentle touch.From daily walks and feeding to grooming and pet sitting, we make sure your furry friend feels safe, loved, and cared for. At Paw Care, we know every pet is unique. That’s why we take the time to understand their needs, routines, and personalities. Whether you're away for a few hours or a few days, you can trust us to treat your pet like family.Because at Paw Care, it's more than just care — it's love with every paw-step.

Veterinary Care at Paw Care Paw Care offers trusted veterinary services to keep your pets healthy and happy. From routine check-ups and vaccinations to illness treatment, our experienced vets provide gentle, expert care tailored to your pet’s needs.

Pet Grooming

Pet Grooming at Paw Care Keep your pet looking and feeling their best with grooming services at Paw Care. From baths and haircuts to nail trimming and ear cleaning, we offer gentle, professional care. Our experienced groomers.
